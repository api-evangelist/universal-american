# Universal American

Universal American was a health insurance holding company focused on providing Medicare Advantage and Medicare Part D prescription drug benefit plans to seniors. The company operated Medicare managed care plans in partnership with Accountable Care Organizations and primary care physicians in Texas, New York, and Maine. Universal American was acquired by WellCare Health Plans in April 2017 for approximately $800 million.
